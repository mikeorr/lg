This is Mars...

Yup, that's Mars all right...

This is a small .gif of a panoramic view of the Martian surface taken by one of the Voyager Landers. It's been graciously provided by the nice folks at NASA who are happily sending all kinds of multi-billion dollar pieces of electronics into outerspace to get us these postcard images.

Pretty cool, eh?

Well, thought you might like to see where all good flames end up... you might as well send it to Mars.
