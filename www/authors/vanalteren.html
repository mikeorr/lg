Ramon lives in Amsterdam, Netherlands where he is employed by a startup and is in charge of the linux administration of a large fail-over cluster. He has been running linux on any device he could get his hands on for as long as he remembers. Currently his favorite linux distro is Gentoo and has been for the last 3,5 years.

When not working he enjoys playing with his cat and spending time with his girlfriend and two children. Apart from that he enjoys sailing on historical sailing ships, although he isn't too picky and vastly prefers sailing over the historical part.
