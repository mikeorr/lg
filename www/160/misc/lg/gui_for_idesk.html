This is really, really funny, for two highly appropriate reasons: 1) I just (re)started using idesk yesterday, and 2) I wrote a very nice GUI for configuring .lnk files. The latter is something I actually did several years ago, but it still works quite well: the links that it creates use the icons from the 'gnome-icon-theme' package, and have the consistent behavior of opening the .lnk file when double-right-clicked. More to the point - since we're very, VERY short of articles this month, I'm going to throw together an article about this exact topic, including installation and usage advice.

So, if you're willing to wait a couple of days - our next issue comes out on the 1st - you'll have access to a very nice GUI for idesk. As far as I know, there isn't one available outside of that.
