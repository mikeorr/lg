The man himself, Richard Stallman. Was was going through his introductory comments at the time I took this picture. Within about 5 minutes after this shot, the room was filled with passioned arguments about free software. I think the English term free, will haunt Richard and the GNU project for some time. The English term free has two meanings, 0 cost and liberty. In other languages, like Spanish, this confusion does not exist since the terms for 0 cost free and liberty free each have their own words. But, this confusion does help fuel the passion of Free Software discussions through the English speaking populations of the world.
