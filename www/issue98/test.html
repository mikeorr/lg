Mike is the Editor-in-Chief of Linux Gazette. You can read what he has to say on the Back Page of many issues. He has been a Linux enthusiast since 1991 and a Debian user since 1995. He was SSC's web technical coordinator 1999-2003, which means he got to write a lot of Python scripts. Now he's involved in three free software projects for Python (Cheetah, Webware and YAML), writes unittests and programs for a Webware e-commerce site, and edits LG from his home. Non-computer interests include wrestling, ska and oi! and ambient music, and the international language Esperanto. He's been known to listen to Dvorak, Schubert, Mendelssohn and Khachaturian too.
