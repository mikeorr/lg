Wacko Topic of the Month

Steve Cody sent in a few more additions to If Operating Systems Ran The Airlines.

Windows 3.0 Air

An old 'air DOS' biplane painted to look like a modern aircraft.

Windows 3.11 Air

As above but fitted with a two-way radio.

Sun Air

An expensive Concorde that travels only between destinations that the average person has never been to and could never afford to go to.

ROM DOS air (a.k.a. TRSDOS, et. all)

A paper aeroplane. Hey, it flies doesn't it? What more do you want?

Altair Air

A pile of timber, wires and nails at the end of the runway, and a nice brochure.
